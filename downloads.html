ILR is partnering with Cornell Print Services for business cards. Currently, Cornell Print Services is set up to create cards using the ILR wordmark only. Through this system, you can enter your contact details into a preformatted card and place your order. Visit Cornell Print Services to get started.

Fonts

Our primary font, Replica, has a very restrictive license that does not allow us to share it with the entire ILR community. Feel free to reach out to us if you have questions.

Our secondary font, Futura Extra Black, has a less restirictive license. If you would like to install it we ask that you email us for further information.

We do offer free, open source options for primary and secondary font use, as well as a system font alternative that is already installed on all computers. Please refer to ourÂ typography section for more information.

Primary font, open source: Roboto
Primary font, system: Arial

Secondary font, open source: Open Sans Extra Bold
Secondary font, system: Arial Black

Letterhead

You can use our Microsoft Word templates to print your own letterhead on your office printer. Please leave the ILR wordmark and Cornell insignia where they are currently placed in the header. You can double click in the footer space to update name and contact information accordingly.

If you are with an institute or otherwise approved sub-brand, please reach out to us to set up a template for you using your institute wordmark. If you are interested in professionally printed letterhead please reach out to us to help with the order.
